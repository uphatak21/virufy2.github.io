Our team

We are all passionate about combining tech with global health, social good, and equity in order to fight the pandemic.

Collaboration

We are a team of over twenty enthusiastic, ambitious youth and students representing 17 universities: California Northstate University School of Medicine, Cornell, Duke, Harvard, Harvey Mudd, Lambda School, Northeastern, Princeton, Stanford, UC Irvine, UCLA, UPenn, UC Santa Barbara, UCSF School of Medicine, York University/Sheridan College, Western University in Ontario, and WUSTL. We’re also worldwide, with members hailing from the USA, Canada, Japan, Nepal, Colombia, Peru, Brazil, Nepal, India, and Pakistan, with a broad global reach.
